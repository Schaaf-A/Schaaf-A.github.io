Scrambled Eggs

Eggs are great. here is a simple recipe to make them even better.

INGREDIENTS

  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tbsp butter
  • 2 tbsp cream

Method

  1. Melt butter into a pan over medium heat
  2. gently mix the eggs and cream in a bowl
  3. Once the butter has melted add the cream and eggs
  4. using a spatula, fold the eggs from the edge of the pan to the center every 20 seconds (if you are making an omlette)
  5. When the eggs are still moist, remove from the heat (it will continue to cook on the plate until served)

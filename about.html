 Hailing from across the country, Anastasia Schaaf is currently located in Janesville, Wisconsin. She was born in Florida and has since lived on both coasts, owing to the fact that her father was in the military. She enjoys traveling and is willing to relocate if the right opportunity presents itself.


 Anastasia is currently attending Craig High school as a sophomore. The focus of her studies has been the creative arts and tech ed. She has been in advanced core classes since third grade, excelling in math and science.


 Anastasia has worked in a variety of conditions. She started “Monarch Forge”, a jewelry business, in 2021 and sold her products at the Kaufman Store’s farmers markets. Recently, she has done contract work with a cleaning company that works with newly built and renovated houses.
 Anastasia has also volunteered in a handful of places, chief among them is her church. Angel Tree is an outreach event she has volunteered for in the past. She has also volunteered at the Humane Society in the past.


 In past years, Anastasia has played tennis on her schools’ teams. She has participates in many extracurriculars including SkillsUSA, Book Club, Engineering Club, Drama Guild, and Dungeons & Dragons. For the last two years, she has helped with the schools’ plays, operating lights and helping with costume organization.
 Outside of school, Anastasia is part of 4H and enters in the annual Rock County fair. She has earned multiple blue ribbons for her entries in photography, Visual Arts, Drawing and Painting, Poultry, and Woodworking.
